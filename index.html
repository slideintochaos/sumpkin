The Sumpkin

Lore

Rooted in forgotten gardens, the Sumpkin grows legs at night and wanders fields, glowing faintly blue. Where it steps, new wild plants erupt overnight. Some claim it can turn barren soil fertile with a single touch.

Field Notes

  • Habitat:Forgotten gardens
  • Primary Ability:Spawns wild plants where it walks
  • Known Weakness:Overly salted soil
